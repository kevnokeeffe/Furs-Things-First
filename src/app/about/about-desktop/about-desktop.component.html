ABOUT US
Hi, I'm Adam
A certified dog trainer. I offer positive reinforcement training for dogs of all ages and levels, from puppy classes to agility courses. I also coach you, the owner, on how to communicate and bond with your dog. The journey begins with training your own dog. Whether you currently own a dog or find one to spend time with, focus on building a regular training routine. Beyond basic commands like “sit” or “down,” teach advanced skills and work on problem behaviors. Contact me for a free consultation and let's start your training journey together!
Vision
At Furs Things First Dog Training, our unwavering vision is to foster a world where every canine companion is not only trained but also treated with the utmost respect. We believe that a well-trained dog is a happy dog, and that harmonious coexistence between pets and their human families is achievable through positive reinforcement and understanding. In this future we envision, dog training transcends mere necessity—it becomes a standard practice ingrained in our society. Pet owners are empowered with the knowledge and skills necessary to forge strong, positive relationships with their furry friends. We see a community where responsible dog ownership is celebrated, and where every interaction between humans and dogs is rooted in kindness, patience, and empathy. But our vision extends beyond the immediate present. We dream of a time when common behavioral problems in dogs are minimized and prevented altogether. Imagine a world where barking, aggression, and anxiety are addressed proactively, leading to a more peaceful cohabitation.
Our vision sees a future where dog behavior problems are minimized and prevented, and where every dog has the opportunity to reach its full potential as a beloved companion. In this utopian canine landscape, every dog—regardless of breed, size, or background—has the opportunity to flourish and reach its full potential as a cherished companion. So, join us on this journey toward a brighter, more compassionate future—one where wagging tails and joyful barks echo in harmony with the laughter of their human counterparts. Together, we can create a world where dogs thrive, families flourish, and love knows no bounds.
