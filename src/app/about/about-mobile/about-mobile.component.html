Vision
Furs Things First dog trainings vision is to create a world where every dog is trained and treated with respect, resulting in happy, healthy, and well-behaved pets living in harmony with their human families. We envision a community where dog training is a standard practice, and where owners are equipped with the knowledge and skills to create positive relationships with their dogs. Our vision sees a future where dog behavior problems are minimized and prevented, and where every dog has the opportunity to reach its full potential as a beloved companion.
